About Me

Jonas I. Marquez graduated from San Francisco State University with his degree in music. He later attended grad school for acting at California Institute of the Arts, but left grad school to persue a carreer as a guitar tech, a job he did for 25 years, touring all over North America and Europe

Jonas was married and had two children. After his wife left him, he was forced to retire from the carreer he loved, in order to raise two kids by himself

Jonas is a self published author, writing two YA Fantasy novels. He is also a 3d modeler and animator. He makes his own videos that he produces entirely, from the modeling to the music and everything in between
